Key Features

Wireless Control

Control your drone wirelessly with ease. Fly it effortlessly using your smartphone or tablet.

Additional Features:

  • Remote Range: 500 meters
  • Real-time Video Streaming
  • Responsive Touch Controls

Snapshot Capture

Capture snapshots with your drone's camera. Take stunning photos and share your aerial perspectives.

Additional Features:

View flight statistics and data. Keep track of your drone's performance and improve your flying skills.

Precise GPS navigation for accurate flight control. Plan routes and explore new locations with ease.

Additional Features:
